GL_HW3_4101056017
=============================

Usage:

```
FOV controls:
key 'w' to move FOV forward
key 's' to move FOV backward
key 'a' to move FOV left
key 'd' to move FOV right
mouse left key to rotate FOV counterclockwise
mouse right key to rotate FOV clockwise

robot and solar control:
key '1' to rotate the sun, long press to keep rotating
key '2' to rotate the plant and the satellite, long press to keep rotating
key '3' to rotate the satellite, long press to keep rotating
key '4' to rotate the thumb, long press to keep rotating
key '5' to rotate the hand, long press to keep rotating
key '6' to rotate the elbow, long press to keep rotating
key '7' to rotate the shoulder, long press to keep rotating
key '8' to make the planet revolve the sun , long press to keep revolving
key '9' to make the satellite revolve the planet, long press to keep revolving
key 'm' to toggle fps and debug message
```
